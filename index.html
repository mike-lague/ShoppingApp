Shopping items are in rows, with alternating backgrounds of pale blue and white.

Hovering over a row exposes a checkmark and an X:

  • clicking the checkmark checks off the item, causing it to be italicized and struck through;
  • clicking the X deletes the item.

Add a new item by entering it in the input field and pushing the Add Item button.
